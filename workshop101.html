Workshop 101: Gender and Sexuality



Details:


Tired of lockdown blue, want to hang loose. Learn something new with some cool folks. Join us for Workshop 101: gender and sexuality.

In a conservative education system, gender and sexuality are often brushed under the carpet. Made to feel as unconversational topics to discuss or to even reflect. Young people don't often get a platform to learn about their own identities.

This workshop will help you learn about yourself. Through this individual learning we strive to empathise with others in this matter. Did you know: suicide rates in lgbt teens are 5 times higher than the heterosexual counterparts.

Join us at the workshop and know more.
