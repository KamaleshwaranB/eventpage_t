Data Analytics Lifecycle - Ecommerce case study



Details:


Data Analytics is the shiny new thing that is hovering on the Horizon. Be it any Organization, a wealth of data is available to consume and act upon. They have to be prepared and understand big data, handle it in real time and derive business insights out of it. This presentation will revolve around the importance of different type of analytics and its lifecycle explained with the help of an ecommerce case study. The session would go detail into the depths of predictive and sentimental analytics needed for customer satisfaction and to prescribe the next best course of action needed to survive in this competitive world.

Speaker - Pavithra, Verizon India.

About the Speaker: I am working as a Data Analyst in Verizon India Telecom Organization. In addition to deriving business insights from customer data, I have also contributed to performing customer profile migrations thereby improving customer satisfaction. I have also delivered emotion and customer sentiment detection algorithms using Python that predominantly use Natural Language processing techniques to improve net promoter score.
